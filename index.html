What's a Blockchain?

Imagine a **digital receipt book** that's shared and verified by a huge community of computers. Every time something happens (like someone minting a Decent Duck), a new receipt is added. Because everyone has a copy, it's practically impossible to cheat, erase, or change a past receipt. This makes it super secure and transparent. That's the blockchain!

What's an NFT?

An NFT (Non-Fungible Token) is like a **unique, digital certificate of ownership** for a specific item, recorded in that shared receipt book. While anyone can look at a picture of the Mona Lisa, only one museum owns the original. Similarly, an NFT proves you own the original version of a digital item, like your one-of-a-kind Decent Duck.

It's all about true, verifiable ownership in the digital world. Still curious? Our QuackBot is happy to answer more questions!

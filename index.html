What's a Blockchain?

Imagine a **digital receipt book** that's shared and verified by a huge community of computers. Every time something happens, a new receipt is added. Because everyone has a copy, it's practically impossible to cheat, making it super secure. That's the blockchain!

What's an NFT?

An NFT is like a **unique, digital certificate of ownership** recorded in that shared receipt book. It proves you own the original version of a digital item, like your one-of-a-kind Decent Duck, and gives you access to our community.
